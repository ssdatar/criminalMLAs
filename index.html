How tainted are Mumbai's legislators?

The Indian state of Maharashtra went to polls in October, 2014. This election was to elect Members of Legislative Assembly (MLAs), the state-level representatives.

This interactive map shows MLAs from Mumbai along with the number of criminal cases and the type of criminal case each legislator has against his/her name. The map also shows how rich each leader is in terms of his/her assets in crore rupees. 1 crore equals 10 million. The colors shown are the party's respective colors.
